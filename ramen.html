Shoyu Ramen Recipe

Shoyu ramen is one of the many different types of ramen dishes. It is flavored with shoyu, which is soy sauce in Japanese.

Ingredients:

  • Dried Kombu
  • Reduced-Sodium Soy Sauce
  • Dry Sake
  • Mirin
  • Boneless Pork Shoulder
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chicken Necks, Backs, or Wings
  • Scallions
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
